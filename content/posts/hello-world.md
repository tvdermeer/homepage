Welcome! I am Thomas van der Meer, and this is my personal website. I built this space to share my thoughts, projects, and the occasional deep dive into topics I care about.

## What You Will Find Here

This site is built around two core passions of mine:

### Artificial Intelligence

I have spent years working with machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. I believe AI should be built responsibly, deployed thoughtfully, and made accessible to everyone. On this blog, I will share:

- Practical tutorials on building AI-powered applications
- Thoughts on the ethical implications of emerging technologies
- Reviews of interesting papers and open-source tools
- Lessons learned from production ML systems

### Golf

Golf has been a part of my life for over fifteen years. It is a game of precision, patience, and continuous improvement — qualities that translate remarkably well into engineering. I will write about:

- Swing analysis and performance tracking
- Course strategy and mental game
- Using data and technology to improve your game
- Golf travel and course reviews

## The Intersection

What fascinates me most is where these two worlds collide. Golf generates an incredible amount of data: launch angles, spin rates, carry distances, strokes gained. AI can turn that data into actionable insights.

I am exploring projects that use computer vision to analyze swings, natural language processing to generate course strategies, and predictive modeling to optimize practice routines.
